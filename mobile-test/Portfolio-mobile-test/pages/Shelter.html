Shelter Dog Drawings

Let's work together! Below you will find a button leading to the shelther approval form. Make sure the shelter or rescue that you are working with knows that you are completing this form. Once your organization is approved an email will be sent with additional information.

The goal for this project is drawing attention to shelter animals while practicing art skills. Animals submitted through this project have the potential to be featured in their own drawings and potentially more!
