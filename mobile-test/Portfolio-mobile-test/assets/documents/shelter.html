Shelter Dog Project

This is going to be a bit of trial and error as I work out this projects details.
For now the process is as follows: Verify Your Shelter: Fill out the verification form below. I will review your shelter for legitimacy. Get Your Link: After verifying your shelter or rescue I will send a private link and additional information so that you can start submitting individual dog photos and bios for me to draw. Recieve Artwork: I will send you copies of drawings for use on your website, social media, or Petfinder profiles! In addition I will begin posting them to my social media for exposure for the animals with the hope that they receive awareness with the end goal of them being adopted!
